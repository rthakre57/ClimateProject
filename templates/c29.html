Flowchart: Differential Equations in Climate Modeling

Physical Basis
Conservation Laws: Mass, Momentum, Energy
Continuity Equation
$$\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (\rho \vec{v}) = 0$$
Momentum Equation (Navier-Stokes)
$$\frac{D\vec{v}}{Dt} = -\frac{1}{\rho} \nabla p + \vec{g} + \nu \nabla^2 \vec{v}$$
Thermodynamic Energy Equation
$$\frac{DT}{Dt} = \kappa \nabla^2 T + \frac{Q}{c_p}$$
Example: Energy Balance Model (ODE)
$$C \frac{dT}{dt} = S(1 - \alpha) - \epsilon \sigma T^4$$
Example: Heat Diffusion (PDE)
$$\frac{\partial T}{\partial t} = \kappa \frac{\partial^2 T}{\partial x^2}$$
Numerical Methods
FDM, FEM, Spectral Methods, Runge-Kutta Integration
